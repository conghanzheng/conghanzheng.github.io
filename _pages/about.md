I am a PhD Candidate at Universitat Autònoma de Barcelona (UAB) and Barcelona School of Economics (BSE) in the [International Doctorate of Economic Analysis (IDEA) program](https://www.uabidea.eu). My supervisors are [Joan Llull](https://joanllull.github.io) and [Hanna Wang](https://sites.google.com/view/hannawang/).

I do applied microeconomic research, with a focus on migration and labor topics. My most recent work is on the household's migration decision, and how it as a whole is affected in the process of migration, especially children and extended families.

My research interests are centered on applied microeconomics, with a particular focus on household decision-making dynamics and the economic consequences of migration. I examine how migration affects not only individual household members, especially children and extended families, but also how these decisions are shaped collectively within the household.”
